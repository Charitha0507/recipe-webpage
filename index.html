A flavorful rice dish layered with spicy marinated chicken and aromatic spices.

  • 2 cups Basmati rice
  • 500g Chicken (bone-in preferred)
  • 1 cup Yogurt
  • 2 large onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 tbsp Ginger-garlic paste
  • Whole spices (cardamom, cloves, bay leaf)
  • Chili powder, turmeric, biryani masala
  • Fresh coriander & mint leaves
  • Saffron soaked in milk
  • Salt & oil as needed

ğŸ‘¨â€ğŸ³ Preparation Steps

  1. Soak basmati rice for 30 minutes and cook till 70% done.
  2. Marinate chicken with yogurt, spices, and ginger-garlic paste for 1 hour.
  3. Fry onions until golden and set half aside for layering.
  4. Cook marinated chicken with tomatoes until half done.
  5. Layer cooked rice on top of chicken in a deep pan.
  6. Add saffron milk, fried onions, coriander, and mint.
  7. Cover and cook on low flame (dum) for 25 minutes.
  8. Serve hot with raita or salan.
